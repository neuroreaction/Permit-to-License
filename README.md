# Permit-to-License
Permit to License helps new drivers and supervising adults track practice driving time without accounts, cloud sync, or unnecessary complexity.
Privacy Policy

Unless otherwise stated in an app-specific section below, apps published by
Cellphone Productions are designed to be local-first.

Our apps do not collect, sell, rent, transmit, or share your personal
information, user-entered data, app-generated records, documents, routes,
logs, notes, images, or other content with Cellphone Productions or third
parties.

Data created inside our apps is stored on the device running the app. Some
apps may allow you to export data, such as CSV or PDF files. When you choose
to export or share that data, you control where it goes.

Our apps do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs,
or external user accounts unless specifically disclosed in the app-specific
section below.

Permissions such as Location, Motion, Contacts, Camera, Photos, or
Notifications are used only when needed for a feature you choose to use.
Denying those permissions may disable that feature, but the rest of the app
will continue to work where possible.

App-specific disclosures

Permit to License

Permit to License stores driving logs, supervising adult information,
approval status, optional GPS route data, and optional motion-derived drive
suggestions locally on your device.

Permit to License does not send this information to Cellphone Productions
or any third party. The app has no account system, no cloud sync, no
analytics, and no advertising.

Location permission is used only if you enable route tracking. Motion
permission is used only if you enable missed-drive suggestions. Exported
PDF or CSV files are created only when you choose to export them.

Winner Circle Lottery Pool

Winner Circle Lottery Pool stores lottery pool members, payment records,
drawing history, tickets, custom lottery/game settings, and related pool
information locally on your device.

Winner Circle Lottery Pool does not send this information to Cellphone
Productions or any third party. The app has no account system, no cloud
sync, no analytics, and no advertising.

Contacts permission, if used, is only used to help you add pool members
from your device contacts. Contact information selected for pool members is
stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to Cellphone
Productions or any third party.

Intent Mark

Intent Mark stores habits, intent counters, timestamps, history, widget
state, and related app settings locally on your device.

Intent Mark does not send this information to Cellphone Productions or any
third party. The app has no account system, no cloud sync, no analytics,
and no advertising.

Widget data is shared only between the Intent Mark app and its widgets on
your device.
